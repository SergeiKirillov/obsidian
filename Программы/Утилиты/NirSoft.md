[NirSoft - freeware utilities: password recovery, system utilities, desktop utilities](https://www.nirsoft.net/)

Here's some examples of what you can find in NirSoft Web site:


- [Password Recovery Utilities](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#password_utils)
    - [WebBrowserPassView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/web_browser_password.html) - View the passwords stored by your Web browser (Supports Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, and Opera)
    - [RouterPassView: Router Password Recovery](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/router_password_recovery.html) - Extract passwords and other information from router backup file (For supported routers).
    - [Mail PassView - Recover the passwords of popular email clients](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/mailpv.html): Outlook Express, MS Outlook, Eudora, Mozilla Thunderbird , and more...
    - [Dialupass: Dialup Password Recovery](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/dialupass.html) - Recovers the passwords of dialup entries (VPN and Internet connections). Works also under Windows 2000/XP.
    - [Network Password Recovery](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/network_password_recovery.html) - Freeware utility that recovers the network passwords stored by Windows XP (Credentials file).
    - [BulletsPassView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/bullets_password_view.html) - Reveal the passwords hidden behind asterisk ('****') or bullets characters in standard password text-boxes.
    - [WirelessKeyView - Recover lost wireless network key stored on Windows](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/wireless_key.html)
    - [PstPassword](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/pst_password.html) - Recover the password of Outlook PST file.
- [Network Monitoring Tools](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#network_utils)
    - [WirelessNetView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/wireless_network_view.html) - View the details of all wireless network in your area (SSID, Signal Quality, MAC Address, and more...)
    - [Wireless Network Watcher](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/wireless_network_watcher.html) - Show who is connected to your wireless network.
    - [BluetoothView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/bluetooth_viewer.html) - Monitor the Bluetooth activity around you.
    - [SmartSniff: TCP/IP Sniffer](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/smsniff.html) - Capture TCP/IP packets on your network adapter and view the captured data as sequence of conversations between clients and servers.
    - [CurrPorts: TCP/IP Connections Viewer](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/cports.html) - Freeware tool that displays the list of all currently opened TCP and UDP ports on your local computer.
    - [AdapterWatch](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/awatch.html) - displays useful information about your network adapters: IP addresses, Hardware address, WINS servers, DNS servers, MTU value, Number of bytes received or sent, The current transfer speed, and more...
    - [DownTester](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/download_speed_tester.html) - Test the [download speed](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/download_speed_tester.html) of your Internet connection.
- [Internet Related Utilities](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#internet_utils)
    - [BrowsingHistoryView: Web Browser History Viewer](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/browsing_history_view.html) - View browsing history of your Web browsers (Firefox,Chrome,IE,Edge)
    - [IPNetInfo](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/ipnetinfo.html) - Find all available information about an IP address: The owner of the IP address, the country/state name, IP addresses range, contact information (address, phone, fax, and email), and more.
    - [MyLastSearch](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/my_last_search.html) - View your latest searches with Google, Yahoo, and MSN.
    - [VideoCacheView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/video_cache_view.html) - Copy video files (.flv and others) from the Web browser cache and temporary folder.
    - [SiteShoter](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/web_site_screenshot.html) - Take a screenshot of a Web site.
    - [DNSDataView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/dns_records_viewer.html) - View the DNS records of specified domains.
    - [IECookiesView: Cookies Viewer/Manager for IE](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/iecookies.html) - View/Delete/Modify the cookies that Internet Explorer stores on your computer. [Read More >>](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/internet_explorer_cookies_view.html)
    - [IEHistoryView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/iehv.html) - View/Delete the URLs that you visited in the last few days.
    - [WhoisThisDomain](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/whois_this_domain.html) - Get information about a registered domain.
    - [DNSDataView - DNS lookup tool](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/dns_records_viewer.html).
- [MS-Outlook Tools](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#outlook_utils)
    - [NK2Edit](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/outlook_nk2_edit.html) - Edit, delete, add, [merge](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/merge_nk2_files.html), and [repair](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/recover_corrupted_nk2_files.html) records in the AutoComplete files (.NK2) of Outlook.
    - [OutlookAttachView - View and extract attachments of your Outlook mailbox](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/outlook_attachment.html).
    - [OutlookStatView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/outlook_statistics.html) - Get statistics about your Outlook mailbox.
    - [OutlookAddressBookView - View or export the address book of Outlook](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/outlook_address_book_view.html).
- [Command-Line Utilities](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#commandline_utils)
    - [NirCmd: Freeware Command-Line Tool](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/nircmd.html) - Do some useful tasks from command-line: turn off your monitor, turn off the computer, open/close the door of your cd-rom drive, dial to VPN/Internet connection, change your display settings, and much more !!
    - [SoundVolumeView - Control the sound volume from command-line or GUI on Windows 10/7/8/2008](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/sound_volume_view.html).
    - [GUIPropView - hide,show,disable,enable,minimize,maximize,resize windows from command-line](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/gui_prop_view.html).
- [Desktop Utilities](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#desktop_utils)
    - [UninstallView - Alternative to the software uninstaller of Windows](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/uninstall_view.html).
    - [SearchMyFiles](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/search_my_files.html) - Alternative to 'Search For Files And Folders' module of Windows and also [duplicate files search](https://www.nirsoft.net/articles/find_duplicate_files.html).
    - [Volumouse](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/volumouse.html) - Control the sound volume with your wheel mouse !!
    - [FileTypesMan](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/file_types_manager.html) - Alternative to 'File Types' manager of Windows.
- [Freeware System Tools](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/index.html#system_utils)
    - [ProduKey - Recover Office/Windows CD-Key](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/product_cd_key_viewer.html)
    - [ShellExView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/shexview.html) - Displays the details of shell extensions installed on your computer, and allows you to easily disable and enable each shell extension.
    - [WhatInStartup](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/what_run_in_startup.html) - Add, delete, modify or disable programs that run at Windows startup.
    - [USBDeview](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/usb_devices_view.html) - View all installed/connected USB devices on your system.
    - [DevManView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/device_manager_view.html) - Alternative to device manager of Windows.
    - [DriverView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/driverview.html) - List all device drivers currently loaded on your Windows.
    - [RegScanner (Registry Scanner)](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/regscanner.html) - Scan and find values in the Registry.
    - [OpenedFilesView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/opened_files_view.html) - View opened/locked files in your system.
    - [SysExporter](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/sysexp.html) - Grab the data stored in standard list-views, list boxes, and combo boxes from almost any application running on your system, and export it to text, HTML or XML file.
    - [BlueScreenView](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/blue_screen_view.html) - View crash information stored in the MiniDump files created on blue screen of Windows.
